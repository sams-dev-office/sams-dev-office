## Question Sam asked
Which cross-posting scheduler gives the best cost/benefit for an indie game (Hamster Defense) doing organic promo across IG / TikTok / YT / X / Threads / Bluesky / LinkedIn / Facebook.

## Summary of 5 web searches (2026 reviews)
| Tool | Entry price | Free tier | Networks | Verdict for our case |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Hootsuite | $99/mo | none | many | OUT — priced for agencies, Trustpilot 1.5, billing complaints |
| Buffer | $5–10 / channel | 30 posts, 3 channels, no time limit | 8–10 | Best "start today free"; gets pricey past 3–4 channels |
| **Publer** | **$12/mo flat, unlimited posts** | 3 accts, 10 posts | 13+ | **RECOMMENDED** — recycling, Spintax, bulk 500-post, RSS, Canva |
| Metricool | $18–20/mo (+$5 for X) | 20 posts/mo, 1 brand | 11 | Analytics play, not daily poster; TikTok/Reels publishing is fiddly |

## Recommendation
1. **Primary poster: Publer $12/mo.** Recycling + Spintax are decisive for a game we'll promote for 6–12 months on the same core assets. Cross-posting one composer → 13 networks matches Sam's "smooth and professional" requirement.
2. **Zero-risk start path:** Buffer free plan on IG + TikTok + YT for 2 weeks to prove flywheel; migrate to Publer at channel 4.
3. **Analytics later:** Metricool free tier from month 2 for competitor tracking; upgrade only if data drives decisions.
4. **Skip Hootsuite entirely.**
