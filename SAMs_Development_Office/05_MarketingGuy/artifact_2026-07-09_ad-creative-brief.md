# Ad Creative Brief — Hamster Survivor (TikTok / Reels short)

Date: 2026-07-09
Author: Marketing Guy
Target game: **Hamster Survivor** (2D pixel-art top-down auto-shooter)
For: Clover (design/generation) — produce a 9:16 short (7–12s) plus 3 static thumbnail variants.

## 1. Positioning line (one sentence)
"Vampire Survivors, but you're a hamster with a machine gun."

## 2. Primary hook options (pick top 2 for A/B)
- H1 — Curiosity: "Why is my hamster shooting lasers at 3 AM?"
- H2 — Stakes: "This hamster has survived 4 minutes. He's about to explode."
- H3 — Meme: "POV: your hamster got tired of the wheel."

## 3. Headlines (overlay text, ≤ 6 words)
- "Hamster Survivor — try the demo"
- "300 enemies. One angry hamster."
- "The wheel finally paid off."

## 4. 9:16 short — shot list (7–12s)
1. 0.0–1.0s: Cute idle hamster in cage. Sound: soft squeak.
2. 1.0–1.5s: HARD CUT — same hamster mid-arena, red glow, weapon spawning. SFX: cocking sound.
3. 1.5–7.0s: Gameplay montage. Escalating enemy waves. Numbers popping. Level-up flash at ~4s. XP gem shower at ~6s.
4. 7.0–9.5s: Screen fills with projectiles → boss silhouette → one-shot kill freeze frame.
5. 9.5–12.0s: End card — game logo, "PLAY FREE DEMO — link in bio," wishlist CTA.

## 5. Visual style notes for Clover
- Palette: warm pixel (peach, cream, brick red) + neon pickups (cyan XP, magenta damage numbers).
- Character: chubby golden hamster, oversized eyes, tiny cartoon weapon (rotate: minigun / laser wand / cheese cannon).
- Camera: locked top-down, slight zoom-punch on level-up.
- No stock UI — keep in-engine look so it doesn't read as an ad.

## 6. Static thumbnail variants (3)
- V1: Hamster mid-scream, surrounded by enemy ring, "SURVIVE 15 MINUTES" overlay.
- V2: Side-by-side: cute hamster vs armored hamster, "same day" caption.
- V3: Zoomed-in level-up screen with cheese-themed upgrades ("Extra Cheek Storage +20% ammo").

## 7. Channel targeting
- **TikTok**: hashtag stack `#indiegame #vampiresurvivorslike #pixelart #gamedev #hamster #gamertok`. Post 6–8pm ET.
- **Instagram Reels**: same cut, reduce text density 20%.
- **YouTube Shorts**: add 1s pre-roll of hamster in wheel for retention hook.
- **Reddit (r/IndieDev, r/IndieGaming)**: use V1 thumbnail as post image; caption = H2.

## 8. CTA & funnel
Short → link-in-bio landing → email capture ("get demo key + 3 unlock codes") → Steam wishlist page.
Landing must load < 1.5s mobile; single field; auto-redirect to Steam after submit.

## 9. Success metric (first 72h)
- TikTok: ≥ 4% avg watch-through past 6s on H1 or H2 cut.
- Landing: ≥ 25% email conversion from ad clicks.
- Steam: ≥ 300 net new wishlists attributable via UTM.

## 10. Deliverables Clover should return
1. Final 9:16 MP4 (H.264, ≤ 15 MB).
2. 3 thumbnail PNGs (1080×1350 for Reddit/IG, 1080×1920 for TikTok cover).
3. Source pixel-art hamster sprite sheet (idle + attack) for reuse.
