## Reddit ads — niche-sub targeting for Steam wishlists
- Platform: Reddit
- Evidence link: https://game-developers.org/indie-game-steam-wishlists-sources ; solo-dev Patrick Tang / This Grand Life 2 postmortem cited in https://presskit.gg/field-guides/how-to-build-steam-wishlist
- Typical CAC: $0.80–$2.50 per wishlist; niche subs (r/capitalismlab) hit 5.8% CTR pulling avg down toward ~$1.10
- Fit for our games/store: STRONG for Hamster Survivor if we find the r/capitalismlab-equivalent niche sub (r/roguelikes, r/tower_defense, hamster/pet-sim subs). Test $50/day per sub, kill anything above $2.

## Meta (Facebook + Instagram) ads — wishlist campaigns
- Platform: Meta
- Evidence link: https://indiedevtool.com/tools/steam-wishlist-cost-calculator/ ; https://www.steampageanalyzer.com/blog/indie-game-marketing-budget
- Typical CAC: $1–$3 per wishlist; ~$14.91 CPM baseline
- Fit for our games/store: MEDIUM — postmortems repeatedly flag Meta wishlist ads as break-even-at-best on <$30 games. Only viable if wishlist→buy converts >12%. Reserve for launch-window retargeting, not cold acquisition.

## TikTok ads — mobile install / awareness
- Platform: TikTok
- Evidence link: https://www.admetrics.io/en/post/tiktok-ads-costs-complete-2026-pricing-guide ; https://www.wordstream.com/blog/tiktok-ads-cost
- Typical CAC: CPI $0.50–$1.80; CPM ~$9.16 (30% below Meta); UGC-style creative 30–40% cheaper CPM than polished
- Fit for our games/store: HIGH for mobile survivor releases and asset-store lead gen. MUST be UGC/native-looking (dev POV, "watch this loop") — do not shoot polished brand video. Test budget: $500–1,000/day/region for validation, per industry standard.

## Ad-creative rule (cross-platform)
- Evidence: TikTok cost guides (admetrics, wordstream)
- Finding: ads with strong watch-through rates cost 40–60% less per conversion than low-engagement creatives
- Fit: brief Clover on hook-in-first-1.5s for ALL video ad creative regardless of platform

## Sober counter-signal — paid wishlists rarely pay back
- Evidence link: https://presskit.gg/field-guides/how-to-build-steam-wishlist
- Finding: On $15 game, $8.50 margin means $1.10/wishlist requires ~1-in-8 wishlist→buy conversion just to break even; most postmortems credit TikTok virality + Steam festivals + Daily Deal over paid ads
- Fit: default posture = ORGANIC first (TikTok + niche subs + Discord). Paid = retargeting + launch-week push only.
