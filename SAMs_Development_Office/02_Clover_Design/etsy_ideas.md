## Rodent Horde — Pixel Enemy Pack (2026-07-09)
- Genre / target buyer: indie devs building Vampire-Survivors-style pixel auto-shooters / roguelike bullet-heavens.
- Contents: 24 32×32 enemy sprites (4-frame walk + 2-frame hit) across 6 archetypes (swarmer, tank, ranged, dasher, healer, mini-boss) + 2 boss sheets, single cohesive 16-color palette.
- Price signal: comparable pixel enemy packs on itch.io tag-enemy/tag-pixel-art cluster around $5–$15; premium palette-locked packs push $20+.
